Frequently Asked Questions on Vlora's Rental

WHAT ARE THE PICK-UP TIMES FOR YOUR VECHIELS?

We are open everyday from 9am to 9pm. Although we are closed at 9 pm, we still deliver you the car you have booked at any time. You just need to contact us about the time of the pick-up and we will be there for you.

HOW LONG IN ADVANCE DO I NEED TO BOOK A VEHICLE?

You can book our cars at any time. You just need to call or contact us on whatsApp and we will see what is available for you.

HOW CAN I PAY FOR THE CAR RENTAL SERVICE?

For your convenience, payments are accepted in person at our center. This ensures a secure and straightforward transaction process, allowing you to settle your rental charges with ease when you visit us.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I RETURN THE CAR LATE?

While we understand that the comforts are hard to let go of, we value punctuality. Hence, if a renter is late in returning the car as per the stipulated deadline, an additional day’s charge is applied as per the category of the vehicle.

CAN AN INTERNATIONAL DRIVER’S LICENSE WORK IN VLORE?

While Albanian Resident Visa Holders only need a valid Albanian Driving License, Visitors from GCC Countries need to provide a valid GCC country driving license. Tourists from countries other than the GCC group need to submit an international driving license to avail of car rental services.
